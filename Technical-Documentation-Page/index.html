Introduction

The Bifrost is a legendary greatsword in Guild Wars 2, it is known for its unique visual effects and has been sought after by many players for its rarity and prestige. In this technical documentation, we will be going over the details of The Bifrost's creation and how to obtain it.

To create The Bifrost, players must obtain its precursor, The Legend, and combine it with other materials through a complex crafting process. The journey to obtain The Bifrost is a long one, but the reward is well worth the effort.

Let's dive into the details.

Materials

Creating The Bifrost requires the following materials:

  • 1 Gift of Mastery
  • 1 Gift of Fortune
  • 1 The Legend (Precursor)
  • 250 Icy Runestones
  • 100 Mystic Clovers
  • 1 Gift of Color
  • 1 Gift of Light
  • 1 Gift of Energy

Some of these materials require specific crafting skills or purchased with in-game currency. It is important to plan ahead and gather the necessary materials before embarking on the journey to create The Bifrost.

Precursor

The precursor for The Bifrost is called The Legend, and it can be obtained through various means, including:

  • Completing map completion of the core game and receiving it as a reward.
  • Purchasing it from the trading post or obtaining it through the Mystic Forge.

Once you have obtained The Legend, you can begin the process of creating The Bifrost.

Recipe

The recipe to create The Bifrost can be obtained from Grandmaster Craftsman Hobbs in Lion's Arch. Once you have obtained the recipe, you can begin the crafting process.

The crafting process involves combining the materials listed above in specific quantities and in a specific order using the recipe for The Bifrost. Once crafted, the weapon has a unique visual effect and can also be upgraded to a legendary precursor, the Zap.

Upgrade Components

In addition to its unique visual effects, The Bifrost can also be upgraded using the following components:

  • Gift of Mastery
  • Gift of Fortune
  • Gift of Magic
  • Mystic Clover
  • Pile of Crystalline Dust
  • Superior Sigil of Nullification
  • Glob of Ectoplasm
  • Obsidian Shard

The combination of these components and the legendary weapon itself will result in an upgraded version of The Bifrost called Zap, which has even more unique visual effects.

Usage

The Bifrost can be used by any character that meets the weapon's minimum level requirement of 80. The weapon can be equipped in the main hand slot and can be used by any class that is able to use a staff. The weapon's unique visual effects can be seen by the user as well as by other players in the surrounding area.

The weapon's skills and damage output are identical to other exotic quality staff weapons of its level. However, the weapon has the added benefit of providing stat bonuses when equipped. The stats provided by The Bifrost are:

  • +104 Condition Damage
  • +73 Power
  • +73 Precision
  • +104 Vitality
Notes

The crafting process for The Bifrost can be a long and expensive process. However, the end result is a visually stunning weapon with unique effects and added stat bonuses. Additionally, the upgraded version of the weapon, Zap, is even more visually impressive.

It is important to note that while The Bifrost provides added stat bonuses, these bonuses may not be optimal for all character builds and playstyles. Players should carefully consider their own character's needs and preferences before deciding to craft The Bifrost.

Overall, The Bifrost is a highly sought-after legendary weapon in Guild Wars 2 and is a testament to a player's dedication and skill in the game's crafting and gameplay mechanics.
