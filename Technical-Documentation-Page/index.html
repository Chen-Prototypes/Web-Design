Introduction

The Bifrost is a cool as heck legendary staff in Guild Wars 2, it's known for its unique visual effects and has been sought after by many players for its rarity and prestige. In this technical documentation, we will be going over the some details and tips of The Bifrost's creation and how to obtain it. If you want the specifics see the wiki.

To create The Bifrost, players must obtain its precursor, The Legend, and combine it with other materials through a complex crafting process. The journey to obtain The Bifrost is a long one, but the reward is well worth the effort.

Let's dive into the details.

Materials

Creating The Bifrost requires the following materials:

  • 1 The Legend (Precursor)
  • 1 Gift of Mastery
  • 1 Gift of Fortune
  • 1 Gift of the Bifrost

Some of these materials require specific crafting skills or purchased with in-game currency. It is important to plan ahead and gather the necessary materials before embarking on the journey to create The Bifrost. For

Precursor

The precursor for The Bifrost is called The Legend, and it can be obtained through various means, including:

  • Having it randomly drop from any bag/chest/container that can drop exotic gear or the randomly obtaining it through the Mystic Forge
  • Completing the associated Legendary Weapons collection achievement for The Bifrost.
  • Purchasing it from the trading post.

It is highly recommended that you simply buy The Legend off the trading post, as obtaining the precursor otherwise can take a very long time through the quests or will require extremely high luck through containers/Mystic Forge .

Gift of Mastery

The Gift of Mastery (GOM) has 2 main parts.

  1. Gift of Exploration
  2. Gift of Battle

Both components take a fair amount of time. The Gift of Exploration oftens puts people off from trying to make their own legendary because it requires you to get 100% Core Tyria map completion (no expansion stuff or living world maps). Most people don't find this very enjoyable and will take around 20-30 hours when doing optimized routing (use a map overlay, like GW2 TacO).

The Gift of Battle can be quite annoying if you don't play World vs World (WvW), depending on your level and how active you are this can also take 10+ hours. This can very daunting if you don't play WvW but many players enjoy WvW and with a bit of effort can be quite accessible.

Usage

The Bifrost can be used by any level 80 character that is able to wield a staff. The weapon's unique visual effects is this rainbow trail effect that can be seen by the user as well as by other players in the surrounding area.

The weapon's skills and damage output are identical to other ascended staff. However, the weapon (and all legendary weapons) has the added benefit of being able to change it's provided stats out of combat. For example, the stats provided by The Bifrost could be (which are identical to Zojja's Spire (berserkers stats)):

  • +251 Power
  • +179 Precision
  • +179 Ferocity
