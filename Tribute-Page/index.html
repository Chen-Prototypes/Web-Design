Steve Jobs

Heres of concise time line of Steve Jobs life:

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco
  • 1968: Gets a job at a HP factory
  • 1974: Works at Atari for a short time
  • 1976: Creates Apple Computer with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne
  • 1977: Releases the Apple II, first big hit for apple
  • 1980: Apple goes public, Steve Jobs networth is around $200 million
  • 1985: Steve Jobs resigns as apple chairmen, then starts NeXT Computer
  • 1993: NeXT switches from selling computers to focus on software, renamed to NeXT software
  • 1996: Returns to Apple
  • 1997: Becomes Apple CEO, Apple introduces the app store
  • 1998: Apple returns to being profitable
  • 1998: Introduces the iMac
  • 2001: Introduces iTunes and later that year introduces the iPod
  • 2003: Introduces iTunes Music Store
  • 2004: Steve Jobs gets surgery to remove a cancerous tumor
  • 2007: Introduces the iPhone
  • 2009: Takes a 6-month leave for medical reasons
  • 2010: Introduces the iPad
  • 2011: Dies due to Pancreatic cancer
